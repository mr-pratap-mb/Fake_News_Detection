About FakeShield v2

A hybrid Fake News Detection Engine

FakeShield v2 operates without relying solely on static datasets. By extracting keywords from your claim, it queries live endpoints (NewsAPI + RSS Fallbacks) to retrieve the latest breaking news. Our custom NLP engine then mathematically evaluates the textual similarity between the initial claim and the live evidence found.
